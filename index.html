The Services We Provide

Remote

We specialize in global recruitment of remote workers for either permanent placement or temporary staff augmentation for as little as 1 month. We additionally provide remote business consulting services.

Onsite

The platform allows clients to filter for talent local to the business. As with all our placements, we offer permanent recruitment, or temporary staff augmentation. Our candidates are vetted, tested, and well trained to ensure optimal client satisfaction.

Hybrid

The Enkaare platform offers businesses the ability to filter specifically for local talent looking for hybrid roles on a temporary or permanent placement. Our in house consulting team also offers all the same hybrid options local to your business.
