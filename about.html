About us

Enkaare is a consulting platform that brings companies and workers on the same platform putting both businesses and candidates in control of the recruitment process. We simplify the hiring process providing a seamless experience that makes the recruitment process transparent, straight forward and more agreeable for all involved.
Enkaare believes that talent exists everywhere across the globe. We take it upon ourselves to identify, nurture and grow that talent so that you can grow your business wherever the business or the talent is to be found.

Our Vision

By finding, nurturing and growing talent across the globe to perform at the optimum in their chosen field, we believe that we will provide the best workforce for a 21 st Century workplace, while ensuring that opportunities do not continue to be limited for talented people merely because of where they are located. This will not only avail businesses to the top tier talent they need to succeed, but also provide lucrative opportunities for people to earn the living they deserve wherever they are.

Our mission

Enkaare seeks to bring businesses across the globe to the best suited workforce regardless of geographical location or time zone. This we do by providing a platform that offers a seamless experience to both businesses and the workforce ensuring that geography is not an impediment to the most ideal workforce for any given job, nor great opportunities for talented professionals wherever they may be.
